About Us

Welcome to the Kenya Police Service website! We are dedicated to serving and protecting the citizens of Kenya.

Our mission is to maintain law and order, prevent crime, and ensure the safety of all individuals in our country. Through this website, we aim to provide information about our services, initiatives, and how you can get involved in supporting our efforts to create a safer community for everyone.
